MRS. SOLNESS. Yes, I suppose I ought to. [She continues to attend the flowers.
SOLNESS.[Bending over the drawings.] Is she still asleep?
MRS. SOLNESS.[Looking at him.] Is it Miss Wangel you are sitting there thinking about?
SOLNESS.[Indifferently.] I just happened to recollect her.
MRS. SOLNESS.Miss Wangle was up long ago.
SOLNESS.Oh, was she?
MRS. SOLNESS.When I went in to see her, she was busy putting her things in order.[She goes in front of the mirror and slowly begins to put on her hat.
